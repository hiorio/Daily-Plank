# Daily Plank Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Daily Plank is designed to help users follow plank workout routines with timers, voice guidance, and local workout history.

## Data Collection

Daily Plank does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with external servers or third parties.

## Data Stored on Device

The app may store the following information locally on the user's device:

- Workout history, such as completed sessions, dates, durations, and completion status
- App settings, such as sound, haptic feedback, and selected voice guidance options
- Temporary workout state used to resume or finish an active session

This information is stored only on the user's device and is not sent to the developer or to a third-party service.

## Account and Login

Daily Plank does not require account registration or login.

## Health, Location, and Contacts Data

Daily Plank does not collect HealthKit data, location data, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, camera data, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Daily Plank does not use advertising tracking, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Voice Guidance

Daily Plank includes pre-generated voice guidance audio files inside the app. The app does not send user text, workout activity, or personal data to a text-to-speech server during normal app use.

## Data Deletion

Because workout records and settings are stored locally on the user's device, deleting the app may remove the locally stored app data.
